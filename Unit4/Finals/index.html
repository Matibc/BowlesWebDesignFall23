J.K. Rowling


Joanne Kathleen Rowling, better known as J.K. Rowling, is a highly acclaimed British author known for her iconic Harry Potter book series. Her writing has captivated the hearts and minds of millions around the world and has achieved widespread critical and commercial success. Her impact on the literature and entertainment industries has been significant, and her influence has extended to various aspects of popular culture.
