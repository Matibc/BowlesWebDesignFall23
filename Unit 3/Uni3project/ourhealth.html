How Does Water Pollution Affect Affect Our Health?

Water pollution can also have serious implications for human health.

Contaminated water can lead to the spread of waterborne diseases, such as cholera, dysentery, and typhoid, which can be life-threatening, particularly in developing countries with inadequate sanitation and clean water access.

In addition, exposure to polluted water over the long term can contribute to chronic health problems such as cancer, reproductive issues, and neurological disorders.
